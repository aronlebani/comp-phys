 Retrograde irregular moon of Saturn. Discovered by B. Gladman et al. 
 (IAUC 7512). Preliminary designation S/2000 S 1 (2000S1). Named in 2003
 (IAUC 8177), from Norse mythology, where Ymir is the ancestor of all the 
 Jotuns or frost giants.
