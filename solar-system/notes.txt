Integration methods
-------------------

Euler - doesn't conserve energy well
Runge-Kutta 4 - better
Leapfrog - special case for x'' = f(x) - great long-term energy conservation

Horizons
--------

Coordinate system:

International Celestial Reference Frame (ICRF)

- Origin at barycenter (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barycenter_(astronomy))

Earth:

3 e v @0 eclip 2024-Apr-01 2024-Apr-10 1d <cr>

Notes:

- Use barycenters, otherwise results contain oscillations of body relative to
  it's barycenter, eg oscillations of earth-moon relative to earth-moon
  barycenter (use 3 instead of 399 for earth).
- If I want to include the moon in the simulation, then use the body centers
  (use 399 for earth).
